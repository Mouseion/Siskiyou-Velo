The Great Outdoor Grill is open 11am to 3pm Monday through Friday, and provides as fast affordable lunch. It's not fast food. It's food that makes your day go fast.

I started the great outdoors grill with the idea of providing a fast, affordable, healthy lunch for people on the go. Start with your choice of California whole grain brown rice or a fragrant jasmine white rice and then we add our ginger citrus marinated chicken and you top it off with your choice of four different sauces.

As someone who loves to spend time climbing, biking, and skiing in the back country I know how good food can taste in the great outdoors. We are located at the corner of Barnett and Medical Park Dr. across from the RVMC Emergency entrance light.
